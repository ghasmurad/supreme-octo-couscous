Run of Show

Keep the energy moving. The host controls the page, teams answer in chat, and the scoreboard keeps everyone invested.

Welcome + Vibe Check — 5 minQuick hello, explain the rules, remind everyone this is low pressure and high fun.
Fun Intros — 15 minName, how long you’ve been here, favorite show/movie/comfort rewatch, and one harmless hot take.
Team Setup — 5 minSplit into 3 teams of 6, pick team names, and choose one person to submit answers.
Competitive Mini Game — 25 minPop culture, majority rules, guess the emoji, and team fun bonus prompts.
Winner + Wrap — 10 minCelebrate the winning team and end with one easy “what are you looking forward to?” prompt.
